Common ancestries

Common ancestries are collectively known as “kith.”

Rare ancestries

These species are less common on Eora. They may have been created through magic, displaced or migrated from other worlds or planes, or slowly evolved through magical means or from different Godlike parents, and developed cultures of their own over time.

Ancestral fey species

Versatile ancestries

Humanoids influenced by the planes, gods, or magic often take on unnatural traits and appearances based on their influence. One may find a human with webbed hands, a goliath whose eyes glow red at night, or a dwarf with copper hair. There are also those with even more fully unnatural appearances such as a dwarf with the eyes, tongue, and skin of a frog, a half giant with red skin and flames for hair, or a human with horns, vestigial wings, and a scorpion tail. Non-kith occasionally take on these influences, but it seems to be exceedingly rare.

If you want to build a character with partial stats of a godlike, pick an archetype, then swap out up to 8 points of traits out of that archetype.

🔮 Godlike

Generally human, dwarven, or half-giants that have been blessed, cursed, or influenced by magic at birth, through magical means, or otherwise created through magic. All godlike share two commonalities: they manifest physical features from their magical heritage, and their eyes are all unnatural in some way.

🧚‍♀️Fey

The chaotic magic of the feywilds tends to affect creatures’ moods, motivations, desires, or dreams more than their physical appearance. Creatures living there tend to have strong emotions, limited inhibitions, and a more mischievous self-absorbed personality than individuals on Terra, as the magic of the feywilds strongly influences characters’ emotions. Physically, the influence of fey magic manifests quickly, affecting creatures living there after about 10 years (fey time).

Fey influences have traits from the “fey” ancestry. 👉

🍄 Shadowborn

The shadowy magic of the fade affects all creatures that encounter it after some time. Much like the magic of the feywilds, the omnipresent magic of the shadowfell infuses creatures born there with shadow and illusion magic.

Shadowfell influences have traits from the “shadowborn” ancestry. 👉
